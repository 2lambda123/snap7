Wrappers

 

This folder contains source interfaces with your applications a.k.a. Wrappers, they are common to all architectures and all OS/Distributions.

You will find these files also into examples folders, they are duplicated to avoid complex references across paths during examples compilation.

If you modify them in examples (you should not) remember to update their copy here.
